AICTE Event: Empowering Young Minds

I was fortunate to conduct a career guidance program at Government Kallar Primary School (அரசு கல்லர் துவக்க பள்ளி) and Government Kallar Higher Secondary School (அரசு கல்லர் மேல்நிலைப் பள்ளி) in Vellaiyammalpuram (வெள்ளையம்மாள்புரம்), engaging with two different age groups.

With the primary school students, the session centered around igniting curiosity and introducing future possibilities through playful, interactive conversations. Their energy and imagination were truly inspiring.

With the higher secondary students, I led discussions on modern career choices, skill development, and adaptability in today's rapidly changing world. We focused on understanding one's strengths and aligning them with emerging opportunities.

This experience reaffirmed my belief in early mentorship as a powerful tool to shape young minds. I'm grateful to the Chairman and Principals of both schools for their support and for allowing me to be part of these students' journeys.
